Our Process...

Questionnaire

Our customers can sign up to take a quiz that asks questions about your skills and interests.

Match

After taking the quiz, our algorithm matches you to a project area with opportunities where your skills will be utilized. In addition, you can filter through your results to find a match that suits you.

Sign Up

Once you are matched to a project you are given the option to sign up to volunteer for that opportunity or donate to the cause.

Service Areas

Health & Medicine

Support efforts to educate the public about health and safety issues, answer phone call at clinics and health hotlines, or running errands at a local hospital. Opportunities available at hospitals, clinics, and assisted living facilities.

Environment

Volunteering opportunities to support environmental conservation efforts. These include marine conservation, jungle conservation, coastline conservation, and sustainable organic farming methods.

Human Rights

Help with efforts to bring basic resources to communities in need such as housing for the homeless and food for the hungry. Or help with global efforts to stop human trafficking and to help with disaster relief.

Immigrants & Refugees

Help refugee youth or help with day-to-day operations at organizations helping with the refugee crisis. Opportunities also include travel to refugee camps to assist those in need.

Animal Rights

Volunteer opportunities to campaign for animal welfare and rights including wildlife conservation and protections against pet abuse.

Education

Efforts to bring education to everyone who lacks this basic need. Opportunities include teaching english, or about nutrition and health.
